## FriendHosting Reviews

FriendHosting maintains a solid reputation among users, particularly for their combination of affordable pricing and reliable support. Here's what customers are saying:

**Positive Highlights:**

The technical support team consistently receives praise for their responsiveness. Multiple users report that tickets are answered "almost instantly," sometimes within minutes rather than hours. This level of attention is somewhat rare in the budget hosting space.

Performance-wise, users appreciate the stable uptime and fast loading speeds. One long-term customer mentioned using the service for over nine years without significant issues. The free site migration service is another frequently mentioned benefit—helpful when you're switching from another provider.

The pricing structure earns positive marks. When users lock in annual or multi-year deals with promotional codes, the value proposition becomes quite compelling. Some report getting VPS plans for $15-16 per year during sales, which includes 1TB of bandwidth.

**Areas for Improvement:**

Like any provider, FriendHosting isn't perfect. A few users have reported occasional setup delays beyond the stated 15-45 minute window, though these seem to be exceptions rather than the rule.

The geographic distribution of data centers, while extensive, may not serve every region equally well. If your primary audience is in Asia-Pacific or South America, you might experience higher latency compared to providers with local presence in those regions.

Some users note that while the control panel options are functional, they'd prefer more choice or the inclusion of cPanel as a standard feature rather than as a paid add-on.

**Overall Verdict from Users:**

FriendHosting scores well on value, support, and reliability. They're particularly popular among users who need European hosting or those running VPN servers, backup storage, or development environments where blazing speed isn't the primary concern but stability and affordability are.

The TrustPilot rating of 4+ stars from verified customers, combined with positive mentions on hosting forums and communities like LowEndBox and LowEndTalk, suggests they deliver on their "hosting for friends" promise more often than not.
